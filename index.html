Rules

  • Change starts with you. Be respectful to everyone in the community. This includes the following:
    1. No racism, sexism, homophobic or xenophobic remarks, joking or not. This includes any derogatory terms.
    2. If you have an opinion, say it respectfully.
    3. Sharing of any personal information without someone's consent is strictly prohibited. (eg. doxing)
  • Absolutely no shitposting.
    1. No unrelated and/or out-of-context memes or images.
    2. No spamming of the same/similar messages, images, gibberish or emojis, regardless of same or across multiple channels.
    3. No distasteful jokes (like 9/11 or Holocaust). Referring to rule 1 here.
    4. No excessive unicode characters, emojis or zalgo text in usernames and / or text.
    5. Spamming bot commands outside of #gazelle-backstage or abusing / exploiting any parts of the bots is forbidden.
  • Politics are strictly banned from the server. Political discussions never end well and no one leaves them happily.
    1. Temporary channels (like #election2016) are excluded from this rule.
    2. The channel description will state if rule 3a applies.
  • NSFW material and discussion is only allowed in the NSFW channel. This also applies to avatars, usernames and nicknames. NSFW specific rules are pinned in the nsfw channels.
  • ZPD decisions are final.
    1. ZPD have full discretion to remove content deemed unpleasant or unwelcomed by community members.
    2. ZPD have full discretion to change channel topic.
