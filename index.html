We aim to be the best place to chat all things Zootopia and meet fellow fans

If you have any suggestions on how to improve the chat, leave it in the feedback channel or send a private message to a member of the Zootopian Police.

If you see unruly or obnoxious behavior in the server, contact a member of the Zootopian Police.

  • Zootopian
  • Verified members of the server.
  • Artist / Writer
  • These are self-assignable roles. With these, you can ask for requests or feedback. Assign them to yourself with .iam writer or .iam artist in gazelle-backstage. Remove them with .iamnot artist and .iamnot writer.
  • Event Groups
  • We have roles for the different events on our server. They are all self-assignable and will result in getting notified for the selected type of event.
  • ZNN Staff / ZNN Head
  • Correspondents of the Zootopia News Network, or ZNN Staff. If you have any feedback, suggestions or content you would like to submit, please use the znn or znn-submissions channel.
  • Role-player
  • Self-assignable role to get access to the role-playing section of the server. Role-playing specific additional rules and etiquette are pinned in the role-playing lobby.
  • Nighthowler
  • Access to NSFW channels. To request access, DM any online member of the ZPD.
  • Zootopian Police
  • Admins of the server. They will be in-charge of ensuring this server remains a peaceful, constructive and enjoyable place for everyone. Temporary channels can be requested from any member of the ZPD if you wish to have a separate channel for discussion. If you find any disturbances in the chatroom, please message a member of the ZPD directly.
