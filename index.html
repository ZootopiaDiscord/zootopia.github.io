Rules

  • Be respectful to everyone in the community.
    1. No racism, sexism, homophobic or xenophobic remarks, joking or not.
    2. If you have an opinion, say it respectfully and back it up with reason.
    3. Don't be a jerk. Keep insults and trash-talking to a minimum and within your close friends.
    4. No shit-talking and gossiping about other server members even in channels that they are not in or viewing.
  • Keep chat clean and civil.
    1. No unrelated and/or out-of-context memes or images.
    2. No spamming of meaningless text, emojis or images. 3 or more lines or images is considered spam.
    3. No pornographic or suggestive material or discussion outside of the NSFW channel
      1. Do not used a cropped version of a pornographic image as your profile image.
      2. If you have to ask yourself if what you want to post is NSFW, the answer is yes.
    4. Politics are strictly banned. Political discussion never end well and no one leaves happy.
    5. Users who are not connected to voice channels are not allowed to have separate conversations within the respective voice text channels.
  • The ZPD are here to help.
    1. ZPD have full discretion to remove content unwelcomed by community members.
    2. ZPD have full discretion to change channel topic.
    3. Do NOT ping the Zootopia Police role except for emergencies such as a raid or troll.
    4. The ZPD will provide a rationale for any actions taken but their decisions are final.
