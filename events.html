Art Challenge

  • What?
  • Members will have the opportunity to show off their art skills with monthly art challenges focused around Zootopia, but with a twist. All challenges will begin at the beginning of the month and should be submitted by the last week of the same month, captioned with #challengeaccepted to tell who is actually participating.
    Note: This is not a competition, but a voluntary activity.
  • When?
  • Starts the beginning of every month or otherwise stated.
  • Where?
  • On the server. Duh. Mainly in #art.
